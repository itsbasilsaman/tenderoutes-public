Tour plan

Our 4-day itinerary is designed to create the perfect romantic escape, balancing special experiences with relaxation time. Here's how your days will unfold:

Day 01
Arrival & relaxation

Morning arrival in Munnar. Check-in to your luxury cottage with valley view. Spend the afternoon relaxing and enjoying the amenities. Evening at leisure to enjoy the property's romantic ambiance and stunning views.

Day 02
Private experiences

Morning private tea estate tour for just the two of you. Afternoon couple's spa treatment at premium wellness center. Romantic candlelight dinner with scenic backdrop near Eravikulam National Park in the evening.

Day 03
Leisure & exploration

Full day at leisure to enjoy the property amenities or explore Munnar at your own pace. Optional activities available (additional cost): private guided tours, photography session, or special romantic surprises.

Day 04
Departure

Enjoy a leisurely breakfast with stunning valley views. Check-out by noon with transfer to departure point. End of services with beautiful memories of your romantic getaway.

Frequently asked questions

Here are answers to some common questions about our Valley of Love: Romantic Getaway package:

What's included in the luxury cottage?

Our luxury cottages feature private balconies with valley views, premium bedding, modern amenities, and romantic decor. All cottages have ensuite bathrooms with luxury toiletries and are designed for maximum privacy and comfort.

Can we customize the candlelight dinner?

Absolutely! We offer various customization options for the candlelight dinner including menu preferences, special decorations, and location choices (additional charges may apply for certain customizations). Please inform us of any special requests at least 48 hours in advance.

What spa treatments are included?

The package includes a 60-minute couple's massage using premium essential oils and techniques. You'll have access to the wellness center's facilities (steam room, jacuzzi etc.) during your visit. Additional treatments can be booked at extra cost.

Is this package suitable for special occasions?

Yes! This package is perfect for honeymoons, anniversaries, engagements, or romantic surprises. We can arrange special additions like flower decorations, cakes, champagne, or photography sessions (additional charges apply). Please inform us in advance about any celebrations.

What's the best time of year for this getaway?

Munnar is beautiful year-round, but September to May offers the most pleasant weather for a romantic getaway. The monsoon season (June-August) has its own charm with lush greenery and misty landscapes, though some outdoor activities may be limited.
