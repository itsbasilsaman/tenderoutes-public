Tour plan

Our 10-day itinerary is designed to showcase the very best of Kerala:

Day 01
Arrival in Kochi

Arrival at Cochin International Airport. Pickup in private Innova Crysta and transfer to Le Méridien. Sightseeing includes Fort Kochi, Chinese Fishing Nets, Jew Town & Paradesi Synagogue, Marine Drive for sunset, and Mattancherry Palace.

Day 02
Kochi to Munnar

After breakfast, scenic drive to Munnar (4-5 hours). En route visit Cheeyappara & Valara Waterfalls and spice plantations. Check-in at Vibe Munnar and relax.

Day 03
Munnar Sightseeing

Full-day sightseeing in Munnar visiting Eravikulam National Park (home of Nilgiri Tahr), Echo Point, Kundala Lake, Tea Museum, and Mattupetty Dam & Lake (boating available). Return to Vibe Munnar for relaxation.

Day 04
Munnar Leisure Day

Day at leisure with optional activities: Tea Plantation Walk & Factory Visit, Ayurvedic Spa & Massage, or personal exploration. Overnight at Vibe Munnar.

Day 05
Munnar to Thekkady

After breakfast, drive to Thekkady (3-4 hours). En route visit Lockhart Gap Viewpoint and spice gardens. Check-in at Cardamom County and relax in the evening.

Day 06
Thekkady Wildlife & Culture

Exploring Thekkady with wildlife and cultural experiences.

Day 07
Thekkady to Alleppey

After breakfast, drive to Alleppey (4-5 hours). Visit Alleppey Beach, Mullakkal Raja Rajeswari Temple, and local handicraft & coir industry. Check-in at Marari Beach Resort.

Day 08
Alleppey Houseboat Stay

Check-in to luxury houseboat at 12 PM. Cruise through Vembanad Lake & backwaters. Enjoy traditional Kerala meals on board, sunset views, and relaxing overnight stay on the water.

Day 09
Alleppey to Kochi

After breakfast, check-out from houseboat and drive to Kochi (1.5-2 hours). Sightseeing includes St. Francis Church (oldest European church in India), Lulu Mall (shopping & food), and Kerala Folklore Museum. Overnight at Le Méridien.

Day 10
Departure

Free time for last-minute shopping or optional spa session. Drop-off at Cochin International Airport. End of services with wonderful memories of Kerala.
