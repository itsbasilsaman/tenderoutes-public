Tour plan

Our 7-day standard itinerary showcases Kerala's highlights with comfort:

Day 01
Arrival in Kochi

Arrival at Cochin International Airport. Pickup in a private Innova car. Transfer to a business hotel. Sightseeing in Kochi: Jewish Quarter & Paradesi Synagogue, Fort Kochi, Chinese Fishing Nets, Marine Drive, Mattancherry Palace.

Day 02
Travel to Munnar

Breakfast at hotel. Drive from Kochi to Munnar (130 km – 4 to 5 hours by car). En route visits: Cheeyappara & Valara Waterfalls, Spice Plantations. Check-in at Fog Munnar Hotel.

Day 03
Munnar Full-Day Tour

Eravikulam National Park (View the Nilgiri Tahr), Echo Point, Kundala Lake, Tea Museum, Mattupetty Dam & Lake (Boating available). Overnight at Fog Munnar Hotel.

Day 04
Travel to Alleppey

Breakfast. Drive from Munnar to Alleppey (140 km – 4 to 5 hours). Visits: Alleppey Beach, Mullakkal Rajarajeshwari Temple, Local crafts & coconut industry. Check-in at Deshadan Hotel.

Day 05
Luxury Houseboat Stay

Check-in to a luxury houseboat at 12 noon. Houseboat Route: Vembanad Lake & backwaters. Traditional Kerala meals onboard. Sunset view & overnight stay on the water.

Day 06
Return to Kochi

Breakfast. Checkout from houseboat & drive to Kochi (60 km – 1.5 to 2 hours). Sightseeing in Kochi: St. Francis Church (Oldest European church in India), Lulu Mall (Shopping & dining), Kerala Folklore Museum. Overnight at business hotel.

Day 07
Departure from Kochi

Free time for last-minute shopping or optional spa session. Drop-off at Cochin International Airport (COK).
