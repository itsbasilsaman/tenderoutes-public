Tour plan

Our 5-day itinerary is designed for family enjoyment with a balance of activities and relaxation. Here's how your days will unfold:

Day 01
Arrival & resort check-in

Morning arrival in Munnar. Check-in to your family-friendly 4-star resort and orientation. Afternoon at leisure to enjoy resort amenities. Evening welcome dinner with local cuisine.

Day 02
Blossom Park & Kundala Lake

Morning visit to Blossom Park with playgrounds and open spaces for children. After lunch, enjoy a peaceful boating experience at Kundala Lake. Return to resort for evening relaxation and dinner.

Day 03
Spice Plantation Tour

Interactive spice plantation tour where the whole family can learn about Kerala's famous spices. Hands-on activities for children. Afternoon free for resort activities or optional local sightseeing.

Day 04
Leisure Day & Local Exploration

Day at leisure to enjoy resort facilities or optional local sightseeing with your personal guide. Evening family games and special farewell dinner.

Day 05
Departure

Morning breakfast at resort. Check-out by 11 AM with transfer to departure point. End of services with beautiful family memories of your Munnar holiday.

Frequently asked questions

Here are answers to some common questions about our Cherished Moments: Family Holiday package:

What kind of accommodations can we expect at the resort?

Our partner resort offers spacious family rooms or interconnected rooms with modern amenities. Facilities include swimming pool, kids' play area, indoor games, and beautiful gardens. All rooms have comfortable bedding, attached bathrooms, and necessary comforts for families.

Are the activities suitable for young children?

Yes! All activities are family-friendly. The park visit includes safe play areas, boating is conducted with life jackets for all ages, and the spice tour has interactive elements for children. The resort also offers kid-specific activities under supervision.

What meal options are available for children?

The resort restaurant offers a children's menu with kid-friendly options at all meals. Special dietary requirements can be accommodated with prior notice. Baby food can be arranged on request (may involve additional charges).

Is there flexibility in the itinerary for elderly family members?

Absolutely. The itinerary is designed to be flexible. Elderly members can choose to participate in activities or relax at the resort as they prefer. Transportation is arranged for comfort, and activities can be adjusted based on mobility levels.
