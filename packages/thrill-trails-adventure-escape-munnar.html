Tour plan

Arrival & orientation

Frequently asked questions

Here are answers to some common questions about our Thrill Trails: Adventure Escape package:

What are the accommodation facilities at the eco-camp?

Our eco-camp features comfortable tents with basic amenities including beds, clean linens, and attached bathrooms. The camp has common areas for dining and relaxation, surrounded by nature while maintaining necessary comforts.

What fitness level is required for these activities?

Participants should have moderate fitness levels. The trek and biking routes can be adjusted based on group ability. Rock climbing includes beginner-friendly options with professional guidance. Those with health concerns should consult a doctor before booking.

What safety measures are in place?

All adventure activities are conducted with certified instructors and proper safety gear. First aid kits are available at all times. We maintain small group sizes for better supervision and adhere to all safety protocols for each activity.

What should we pack for this trip?

Essential items include comfortable trekking shoes, weather-appropriate clothing (layers recommended), sunscreen, water bottle, small backpack, personal medications, and insect repellent. We'll provide detailed packing list upon booking confirmation.

When is the best time to see Neelakurinji blooms?

Neelakurinji flowers bloom once every 12 years (next expected bloom 2030). However, we offer alternative scenic treks year-round with similar difficulty levels and beautiful views. During non-bloom years, we focus on other unique aspects of Munnar's landscape.
