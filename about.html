Help & FAQs
Can I customize my Kerala itinerary?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating personalized Kerala experiences. Our travel designers will work with you to craft an itinerary that matches your interests, pace, and budget perfectly.

When is the best time to visit Kerala?

Kerala is beautiful year-round! September to March offers pleasant weather for beaches and backwaters, while April-August is perfect for Ayurvedic treatments and monsoon experiences. We recommend booking at least 3 months in advance for peak season (December-January).

What payment options do you offer?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and installment plans (0% interest available). For Middle Eastern travelers, we also accept payments through local banking partners for your convenience.

Do you arrange airport transfers in Kerala?

Yes, we provide private, comfortable transfers from all Kerala airports (Kochi, Trivandrum, Calicut) to your accommodation. Our English/Arabic speaking drivers ensure a smooth arrival experience.

Are your houseboats safe for families?

Absolutely. Our premium houseboats feature safety railings, life jackets for all ages, and trained staff. Many families with children enjoy our houseboat experiences - we can arrange special kid-friendly meals and activities too!
