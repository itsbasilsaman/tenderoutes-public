7-Day Kerala Tour Package

Experience God's Own Country - From Tea Plantations to Backwaters

7 Days
5+ Destinations

Explore the breathtaking landscapes of Kerala with our carefully curated 7-day tour package. From the misty hills of Munnar to the serene backwaters of Alleppey, experience the best of South India's natural beauty and cultural heritage.

Your Kerala Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Kochi

Welcome to Kerala! Upon arrival at Cochin International Airport, you'll be greeted by our representative and transferred to your hotel.

Accommodation: Holiday Inn, Kochi
Airport transfer included
Day 2

Drive to Munnar

After breakfast, drive to Munnar (130 km, approx 4.5 hours) through scenic routes with rolling hills and tea plantations. Check in to your resort and spend the evening at leisure.

Accommodation: Valley by Kondotty, Munnar
Distance: 130 km (4.5 hours)
Day 3

Explore Munnar

Full day sightseeing in Munnar visiting Eravikulam National Park (home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr), Tea Museum, Mattupetty Dam, and Echo Point. Enjoy the cool climate and breathtaking views.

Accommodation: Valley by Kondotty, Munnar
Highlights: Tea plantations, wildlife, scenic views
Day 4

Drive to Thekkady

After breakfast, drive to Thekkady (90 km, approx 3.5 hours). Visit Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary and enjoy a boat ride on Periyar Lake to spot wildlife like elephants, tigers, and various bird species.

Accommodation: Greenwoods Resort, Thekkady
Distance: 90 km (3.5 hours)
Day 5

Drive to Alleppey

Morning drive to Alleppey (150 km, approx 4.5 hours). Check into your houseboat for an overnight backwater cruise. Enjoy Kerala's famous backwaters, passing through palm-fringed canals and observing village life along the banks.

Accommodation: Lake Canopy Houseboat, Alleppey
Distance: 150 km (4.5 hours)
Day 6

Return to Kochi

After breakfast, disembark from the houseboat and drive back to Kochi (60 km, approx 1.5 hours). Spend the afternoon shopping for souvenirs and exploring local attractions like Fort Kochi and the Chinese fishing nets.

Accommodation: Radisson Blu, Kochi
Distance: 60 km (1.5 hours)
Day 7

Departure from Kochi

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and transfer to Cochin International Airport for your onward journey, taking with you wonderful memories of Kerala.

Airport transfer included
